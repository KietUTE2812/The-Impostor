## 1. Game Objective

| Role | Objective |
|------|-----------|
| **The Players (Crewmates)** | Must work together to identify and vote out the Impostor |
| **The Impostor** | Must hide their identity, pretend to know the secret Keyword, and survive the final vote |

---

## 2. Setup

### Players
**4 to 8 players** are required.

### Assign Roles
The game will secretly assign **one player** to be the **Impostor**. All other players are **Crewmates**.

### Distribute Words

1. The system selects one **Category** (e.g., "Animals") and one secret **Keyword** from that category (e.g., "Penguin")
2. **Everyone** is shown the **Category** ("Animals")
3. **Crewmates** are also shown the secret **Keyword** ("Penguin")
4. **The Impostor** does **not** know the Keyword, only the Category

---

## 3. Gameplay (The Rounds)

The game is played over **several rounds** (e.g., 3 rounds, depending on the player count).

- In each round, **every player takes a turn** saying **one single word** that relates to the Keyword
- The turn order may be shuffled each round

## 4. Voting

After all rounds are complete, the game enters the **discussion and voting phase**.

1. Players will debate who gave the "strangest" or most suspicious-sounding words
2. Each player casts **one vote** for the person they believe is the Impostor
